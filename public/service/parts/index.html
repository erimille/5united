Parts

Here's a list of parts suppliers who can help you keep your BMW motorcycle running. Many of the companies listed have been used by members of 5United at various times. The listed vendors have consistently proven their customer service is excellent. If you can't find it from one of these vendors, it may not exist.


Hucky's
440 12th ST SW, Vero Beach, FL 32962
772 564 9589

aka Hans Lowe

Eurotech Motor Sports
1017 Willow Street, Chico, Calif.
(530) 345 7145.

Chris Canterbury has supported 5 United for many years. Very knowledgeable about /5s and you probably can't restore a /5 without him. Order parts online for all European bikes.

Rocky Point Cycle
2509 Linebaugh Road, Xenia, OH., 45385-9512
(937) 376-9792

Excellent source for ignition switch replacement kit, stainless steel fastener kits, stainless steel exhausts, Boyer electronic ignition, silicone gaskets, Mikuni carburetor conversions and many other parts, including stuff for British motorcycles.

MPH Cycles, Inc.
16510 Park Row, Houston, TX 77084
(281)647-6557 (281)579-8885 Fax (281)579-9035

Mike Haven left 5 United when his interest in motorcycles and running a business consumed him. A glutton for punishment, he opened a motorcycle shop in Houston. In addition to servicing /5s and other BMWs, he sells Moto-Guzzi and MuZ motorcycles. He also has NOS (new old stock) BMW parts. You'll like him.

Craig "Vech" Vechorik is well-known in BMW circles for his vintage restorations. He also sells the "E-Z Clutch." This product makes the clutch easier to work on a /5 (or other BMWs). It's quite possible that you can't restore a pre-/5 BMW without talking to Vech. Should you stray down the dark path (the one without oil pumps), you need to know about Vech.

Parts Catalog

Reprint of the original /5 parts catalog, complete with sections and tabs. It's one of the best print references for /5 restorations. This book was available in hardcopy (with thanks to club member Trig Haroldson) but is now only available online. For the curious, this email thread provides more context.
